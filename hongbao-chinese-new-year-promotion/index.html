什么是纸钱包？

纸钱包只是一个公共地址，并且是印在纸上的对应私人密钥。您可以将资金发送到公共地址，然后通过扫描私钥访问余额。这是一种物理存储比特币现金并与您喜欢的人共享的独特方法。

What is a paper wallet?

A paper wallet is simply a public address and a coresponding private key printed on a piece of paper. You can send funds to the public address and then access the balance by scanning the private key. It is a unique way to physically store Bitcoin Cash and share it with whoever you like.
